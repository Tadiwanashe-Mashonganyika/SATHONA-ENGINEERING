ABOUT US

Est 2022

Our Story

At Sathona Engineering, we don’t just build structures; we create legacies. Since our inception in early 2022 and incorporation in first quarter of 2023, we’ve been shaping skylines, fostering communities, and leaving footprints of progress.

  • Integrity is paramount, we operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability, fostering trust internally and externally.
  • Safety is of utmost importance at Sathona Engineering. We prioritize it in all operations, following strict protocols to prevent accidents and injuries.
  • Satisfying client needs whilst not harming the environment we operate.

Company Background

Our Genesis

Founded by visionary engineers Ronald Ngomani and Martin Kapenda, Sathona Incorporated began with a single residential project, Emerald Hill. Today, we have expanded into commercial spaces and infrastructure development, driven by passion and purpose.
